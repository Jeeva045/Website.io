What services do you provide?

1

Our specialty is creating user-focused web and app designs, informed by extensive industry knowledge. We also support your brand's growth with a suite of collateral services, from email templates and social media graphics to business cards and branding essentials.

How can I get started with DesignCraft.me?

1

It's simple. Get started by selecting a design package or scheduling a quick 15-minute call to discuss your needs. As a member, you'll have a personal task management system to submit and monitor all your requests with ease.

What is your communication process for projects?

1

We operate asynchronously, using Trello & Figma for all project communications. Updates and insights are shared via Loom videos with deliverables, ensuring clarity and continuous engagement.
